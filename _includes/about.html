About

I'm a computational biologist with a wet-lab background in genetics, genomics and developmental biology. I'm currently employed as a bioinformatics postdoc at the Friedrich Miescher Institute in Basel, where I collaborate with colleagues from the Marc Buehler and Helge Grosshans labs.

My scientific interests center around understanding gene regulation from a genomic perspective. I work or have worked with transcription factor binding and epigenetics (ChIP-seq, DamID), gene expression (RNA- and small RNA-seq), evolutionary and other functional genomics data (DNase-seq, FAIRE-seq, ribosomal footprinting, etc.).
